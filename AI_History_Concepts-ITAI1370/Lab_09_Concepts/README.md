# AI Concepts Lab 09: Historical AI Architectures

## Lab Overview
This lab provides a deep dive into the historical milestones of Artificial Intelligence, focusing on early symbolic logic and the transition to neural networks.

## Key Topics Covered
* ELIZA and Early Natural Language Processing
* * The "AI Winter" and its causes
  * * Perceptrons and the birth of Connectionism
    * * Knowledge-Based Systems (Expert Systems)
     
      * ## Observations
      * * Early AI systems were limited by rigid rule-sets and computational power.
        * * The shift from symbolic AI to neural networks allowed for better handling of ambiguity.
          * * Modern AI still uses concepts from early symbolic logic (e.g., knowledge graphs).
